PlayStation 4 Interface Redesign

A first peak of the importance of UX design in everyday life.

In this course we are taught about the process of UX design from starting from an idea to implementation. For our group project, we were to find a problem that we found in an interface (ranging from physical things like an elevator button interface to an online application). Our group had a slight problem with the PS4 menu interface as well as the lack of use of the touchpad on the controller.

On the right are some picture form our low fidelity prototype.

As we already know at the beginning of the project that we only have time for medium fidelity prototype, we seek the process of the redesign process very important. We brainstorm very hard, assist by the 10 plus 10 design method, which we came up with couple different style of design, and pick our favourite one to draw alternative design based on that one concept.

We pick the grid design as our final choice because user already very familiar and could naturally adapted using the grid based interface like smart phone.

And then we did heuristic evaluation within the group, reflect the result to our medium-fidelity prototype as the video showing below.

Our medium fidelity built with Unity3D using an Apple MacBook Pro's touch pad to simulate the touchpad of the DualShock 4 controller.

This video uses a Wizard of Oz approach showing what the functionality of the touchpad on the controller could do in a future implementation.
